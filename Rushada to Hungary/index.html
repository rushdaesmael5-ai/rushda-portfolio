My Teaching Journey

I started teaching young adults in my community how to code and do graphic design. Teaching taught me patience, listening, communication, and adaptability.I started teaching young adults in my community how to code and do graphic design, and soon enough I found out that teaching is not as easy as it seems. When I did go too fast and lost a few of the students, I eventually learned to slow down, be patient, and adapt. In this way, it taught me about listening, communication, and patience, and at the same time, challenges were opportunities for me to learn and grow, while helping others learn more effectively.
